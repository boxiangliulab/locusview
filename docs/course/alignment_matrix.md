## Constructive-alignment checks (per the framework)

1. **Every ILO is heavily targeted (a `3`) somewhere** — ✅ ILO1→M0, ILO2→M0/Portfolio, ILO3→M1,
   ILO4→M1/Milestones, ILO5→M2/Portfolio/Final, ILO6→M1/Portfolio/Milestones/Final.
2. **Every assessment column has a non-zero cell** — ✅ Portfolio, Milestones, Journal, and Final
   defense each target multiple ILOs.
3. **No module column is entirely zero** — ✅ every module advances at least two ILOs.
4. **Balance** — ILO6 (create & document) and ILO5 (evaluate agents) carry the most weight, which is
   intentional: *building a real, reviewed feature with agents* is the course's signature outcome.
   ILO1 (explain the lifecycle) is front-loaded in M0 and revisited in the journal — deliberate, since
   it's the conceptual scaffold the doing hangs on.
